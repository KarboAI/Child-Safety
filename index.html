KarboAI Safety Policy & Child Protection Standards

Our app is designed for safe communication, voice chats, and AI-powered role-play interactions. We enforce strict policies to protect users—especially minors—from exploitation, abuse, and inappropriate content.

Prohibited Content & Behavior

Sexual Content

We strictly ban any sexual content (text, audio, images, or AI-generated material).

Child Exploitation

Zero tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), grooming, or predatory behavior.

Harassment

No harassment, bullying, doxxing, or threats of any kind.

Hate Speech

Prohibition of hate speech, racism, extremism, or illegal activities.

Privacy Violations

Sharing personal data (e.g., phone numbers, addresses) in public chats is forbidden.

Safety Measures for Minors

Age Restrictions

The app is rated 12+. Users under 13 must not misrepresent their age.

AI Moderation

Automated filters block explicit keywords and report violations.

Human Oversight

Moderators review reports and high-risk chats (e.g., voice channels).

Reporting Tools

Easy-to-use "Report User" feature and email reporting options.

Privacy Controls

Limits on private chats for underage users to prevent unwanted contact.
